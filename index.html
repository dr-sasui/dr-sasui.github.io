General Medicine

Comprehensive primary healthcare with expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of medical conditions

Dr. Sasui provides complete outpatient consultations, handling diverse medical cases with over 3 years of clinical experience. Specializing in routine check-ups, chronic disease management, and preventive care, she ensures personalized treatment plans for each patient. Her approach focuses on building lasting doctor-patient relationships while maintaining the highest standards of medical care.

Emergency Care

ACLS certified emergency physician with extensive experience in critical care and trauma management

Dr. Sasui is highly skilled in handling critical emergencies including cardiac arrests, trauma cases, and life-threatening situations. With ACLS certification and following established trauma protocols, she provides immediate stabilization and coordinates with medical teams for comprehensive emergency management. Her experience includes working in high-pressure emergency environments and managing complex medical emergencies.

Pediatrics

Specialized care for children and adolescents with extensive training in pediatric medicine

Dr. Sasui has completed comprehensive training in pediatrics during her house job at Mayo Hospital, Lahore. She provides complete healthcare services for children, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, growth monitoring, and treatment of childhood illnesses. Her gentle approach and understanding of pediatric care ensures comfortable and effective treatment for young patients and peace of mind for parents.

Gynecology & Obstetrics

Women's healthcare specialist with experience in gynecological conditions and obstetric care

Dr. Sasui has completed extensive training in gynecology and obstetrics, including hands-on experience with normal deliveries and women's health issues. She provides comprehensive gynecological consultations, prenatal care, and has assisted in various obstetric procedures. Her compassionate approach ensures comfortable and confidential care for women's health concerns throughout all stages of life.

Preventive Care & Health Education

Promoting wellness through preventive healthcare and comprehensive patient education programs

Dr. Sasui is passionate about preventive healthcare and patient education. She conducts regular health screenings, corporate employee checkups, and develops medical awareness content. Her commitment to community health includes participating in free medical camps and creating educational videos to promote health awareness. This proactive approach helps patients maintain optimal health and prevent disease progression.
